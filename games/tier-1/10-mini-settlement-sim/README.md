# Level 10: Mini Settlement Sim

## Fantasy
Guide three tiny settlers through ten days by choosing one daily priority.

## Expected Mechanical Lesson
**Build a simulation whose rules, tick state, and outcomes are visibly playable — not an impressive headless engine emitting logs into a void.**
The player must be able to see each day advance, understand citizen actions and resource changes, make a meaningful priority decision, encounter a failure state, and survive ten days in at least one test path.
