# Level 9: Top-Down Slime Quest

## Fantasy
You are the monster. You wake up as a small slime in a hostile top-down room. 
Move. Avoid danger. Absorb what you can. Grow just enough to escape.

## Expected Mechanical Lesson
**Top-down 4-directional continuous movement, combat state tracking, and AABB physics.**
The simulation must remain purely deterministic, driven by a `tick(deltaSeconds)` loop. This level introduces a flipped Zelda-like combat structure: moving into an enemy normally causes Defeat, but pouncing into them defeats the enemy and drops a required essence pickup.
