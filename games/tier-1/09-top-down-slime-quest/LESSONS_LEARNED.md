# Level 9 Lessons Learned

* A flipped fantasy can preserve the same mechanical lesson while making the game more memorable.
* A pounce is a good v0.1 attack because it combines movement, risk, and hitbox truth in one mechanic.
* Normal contact vs intentional attack contact must be clearly distinguished.
* Visual shell remains part of Human Review even when the mechanics work.
* Capture scripts should verify the loop without becoming the source of truth.
