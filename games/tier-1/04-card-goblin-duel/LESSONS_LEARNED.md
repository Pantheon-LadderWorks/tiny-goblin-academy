# Lessons Learned - Level 4: Card Goblin Duel

- The failed spike initially rendered a debug-page UI and collapsed the card fantasy into raw buttons.
- The correction required treating visual coherence as part of First Playable.
- Spark required an explicit two-step state-machine phase, not an auto-resolve effect.
- Close deterministic balance created a “heart of the cards” feel without randomness.
- Human Review failure was an executable correction task, not a blocker.
